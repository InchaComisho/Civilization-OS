## 3. Connection to the Four Planetary Circulations

The central target of Civilization OS rewrite is the recovery of the four planetary circulations:

```text
1. Water phase-transition circulation
2. Atmospheric circulation
3. Ocean circulation
4. Food and organic matter circulation
```

### 3.1 Water Phase-Transition Circulation

Water evaporates, forms clouds, returns as rain, infiltrates soil, is absorbed by plants, and returns to the atmosphere through evapotranspiration.

This is the core of Earth’s natural cooling function.

### 3.2 Atmospheric Circulation

Atmospheric circulation connects water vapor, clouds, rainfall, winds, updrafts, and heat transport.

Forest loss, urban heat accumulation, dryland expansion, and evapotranspiration decline alter the movement of water vapor and heat into the atmosphere.

### 3.3 Ocean Circulation

Ocean circulation includes currents, vertical mixing, heat transport, nutrient cycling, oxygen cycling, and carbon cycling.

If heat accumulates in the surface layer and stratification increases, vertical circulation weakens, affecting phytoplankton, carbon fixation, and oxygen supply.

### 3.4 Food and Organic Matter Circulation

This circulation connects plants, food, fallen leaves, food residues, waste, livestock manure, microorganisms, humus, soil, and crops.

When this circulation is broken, soil microorganisms, water retention, humus, carbon fixation, evapotranspiration, and food production weaken together.
